Presidential Remark

It is my utmost pleasure to welcome you to the official website of the Road Transport Employers Association of Nigeria (RTEAN), the foremost Road Transport Union in Nigeria. Here, you will find updated information about RTEAN and our activities across Nigeria. As a Union backed by Law (Read About Us), our administration is committed to the Transformation and Reformation of the transportation sector of Nigeria’s economy. My team and I are determined to pursue these goals with all the necessary and required vigor, with the collaboration of government at all levels and relevant stakeholders to ensure the actualization of our mandate within a reasonable time. These, we are doing, simply because we are concerned about your welfare, wellbeing and comfort. May I therefore, use this opportunity to specially invite all well meaning individuals and corporate bodies to join us in our bid to contribute our quota to the development of our Country, Nigeria through the transportation sector.
God bless Nigeria

Sincerely Yours
Alhaji (Dr) Musa Muhammed
(Maitakobi 1)
Executive National President, RTEAN.
