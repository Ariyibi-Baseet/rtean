Nigerian Youth Congress Leadership Visits Ekiti State

The Leadership of the Nigerian Youth Congress led by the President, Blessing Akinlosotu Visits Ekiti state.

The President met with the Senior Special Assistant to the Governor on Youth and Students Affairs Hon. Moses Ademiloye who represented the Governor.

The Governor declare full support for the Nigerian Youth Congress and promised to work very closely with the Leadership in the State for the development of the Ekiti Youths.
