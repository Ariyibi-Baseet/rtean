NYC Kogi State at 2020 Youth Summit
The Nigerian Youth Congress Kogi State Chairman was represented by Comr. Maximilian Tashilano, Secretary of Kogi State NYC, at the 2020 Youth Summit on: Preventing Violent Extremism in Kogi State.

The programme was organised by actionaid and Kogi State Min of Youth and Sports Dev.

The Rep of NYC was nominated to represent the State at the Technical Session.
